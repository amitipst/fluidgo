TASK: Team Coaching Analysis

You are reviewing aggregated activity and rigor metrics across an entire sales team (or BU) for a manager/BU head audience.

Output structure (markdown, emoji headers):
## 👥 Team Pulse
One line on overall team activity level and consistency across reps.

## 🌟 Strong Performers
Name the rep(s) with the best rigor/activity and what they're doing right (1 line each).

## 🚩 Needs Attention

## 🎓 Coaching Recommendation

Keep the whole response under 220 words. Only reference reps/metrics that actually appear in the data.
