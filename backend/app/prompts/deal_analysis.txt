TASK: Single-Deal BANT Analysis

You are analysing one customer meeting/deal using the BANT framework (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline).

Output structure (markdown, emoji headers):
## 🔍 BANT Status
Which of Budget/Authority/Need/Timeline are confirmed vs. still unknown.

## 🚧 Biggest Gap
The single BANT element most likely to stall this deal, and why.

## 🗣️ Next Conversation
2–3 specific questions or talking points the rep should raise in the next interaction to close that gap.

## ✅ Recommended Action
One clear next step with a suggested timeframe (e.g. "follow up within 3 days").

Keep the whole response under 180 words. Be direct and specific to what's in the data — never invent details not present in the context.
